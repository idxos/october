Charades Against Civilized Society!


Press the button below to get a quick rundown about how to play, or click cancel to skip start playing now.

To play the game,
First players group into two teams, and choose one person to be captian for each team.

Next hand the device to the Captian of Team 1.
They will be prompted to enter the teams basic info, and player names.
When Team 1 is finished, hand the device to the Captian of Team 2 and enter info for their team.

When complete had the device back to Team A's Captian and
click the 'Start Game' button to begin.

Each team in turn will get to choose a "Secret Phrases" word or phrase on the device. When ready to begin actin out the "Secret Phrase" click the "Go" button.

The actor then has 60 seconds to act out the "Secret Phrase."
The guessers attempt to guess the word or phrase based on the actor's performance. They can ask questions, to which the actor may give non-verbal responses, such as nodding in affirmation.
If any of the guessers says the correct word or phrase within the time limit, Click the "Got it!" button.

If the phrase is not guessed correctly in 60 seconds, the players from the opposing are given the option to choose click the "Steal" button, and may make one guess as to what the "Secret Phrase" is.
Guessing correctly results in one point for the team, if not correct the team attempting the "Steal" loses a point.

The teams alternate until each team member has had an opportunity to be the actor.
Fist team to 10 points, wins!
